The Work Shirt

We created this shirt for women like us.

Tech tinkerers, makers, doers, experimenters, hobbyists, stitchers, tailors, painters, woodworkers,
metal-smiths, jewelers, crafters, and anyone and everyone who has put together Ikea furniture or painted a wall.
It is made from durable and beautiful material that will wear in over time instead of wearing out.

100% Cotton Canvas • Corozo Buttons • Chambray Trim • Made in America

Top Pockets

Large enough for business cards, credit cards, cash, and/or a pocket square!

Bottom Pockets

Garments for women never have enough pockets. The bottom pockets on this Work Shirt are the perfect size to fit your smart phone or a small moleskin.

Shirt-Style Hem

This shirt is fitted to female curves. It has darts in the front and back panels to create shape and has a shirt-style hem for coverage.
